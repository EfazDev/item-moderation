Item Moderation Scanner is a Discord bot designed to notify users if a Roblox bundle or UGC item has been successfully approved or declined by Roblox Moderation!

Features:

  • Scan items including bundles and get pinged when approved! (Specialty)
  • Timed Item Approval Leaderboards (Shortest and Longest)
  • Check if Item or Bundle is Approved (Command)
  • Check your Active Items on the list! (Command)
  • Item Scan Stats (by Items in the Scanner only)
  • Lock Accounts to you (Application required)
  • Special Scanners and Registers (Application required)
